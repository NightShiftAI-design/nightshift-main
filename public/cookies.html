Cookie Policy

How NightShift AI uses cookies

Last updated: January 2026. This policy explains what cookies are, what we use them for, and how you can manage them.

✔ Privacy-first ✔ No behavioral ads ✔ No cross-site tracking ✔ Clear controls

Summary

We use cookies and similar technologies only for basic site functionality and limited analytics. We do not use cookies for behavioral advertising, and we do not sell visitor data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, keep sessions stable, and understand usage patterns.

How we use cookies

  • Basic website functionality and performance
  • Security and reliability (for example: rate limiting / abuse prevention)
  • Understanding which pages are visited most (aggregated analytics)
  • Improving content and user experience

What we do not use cookies for

  • No behavioral or targeted advertising
  • No cross-site tracking for profiling
  • No selling or renting of visitor data
Cookie types we may use Essential + analytics

Essential cookies: required for core site functions (navigation, form submissions, security features).

Preference cookies: remember optional choices (for example: theme preference like dark/light).

Analytics cookies: help us understand usage patterns in aggregate so we can improve pages and content. These are not used for advertising and are not intended to identify you.

Third-party services Limited

We may use hosting, security, or basic analytics providers that set limited cookies necessary for reliability and performance monitoring. These cookies are not used for advertising or cross-site profiling.

If we ever add additional analytics tools that change how cookies are used, we will update this policy and (where required) provide a clear choice mechanism.

Managing cookies in your browser Your control

Most browsers let you control cookies through settings. You can typically:

  • View which cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (may affect site functionality)

Disabling cookies may impact some features (for example: remembering your theme preference).
