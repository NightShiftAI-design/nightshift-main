How We Use Cookies

Last updated: January 2026. This Cookie Policy explains how NightShift AI uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and understand how visitors use the site.

How NightShift Uses Cookies

  • Basic site functionality and performance
  • Understanding which pages are visited most
  • Improving content and user experience

What We Do NOT Use Cookies For

  • No behavioral or targeted advertising
  • No selling or renting of visitor data
  • No tracking across unrelated websites

Third-Party Services

We may use basic analytics or hosting services that set limited cookies necessary for site reliability and performance monitoring. These are not used for advertising or profiling.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how certain parts of the website function.
