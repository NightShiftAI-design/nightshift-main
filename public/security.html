Security

How We Protect Your Data

NightShift AI is designed for hospitality operations where guest privacy, payment safety, and reliable escalation matter. We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data.

No Payment Cards Collected

NightShift does not collect or store guest credit or debit card numbers through AI voice agents. Payments are routed through your existing secure processes.

Secure Cloud Infrastructure

Systems run on secure cloud providers with encrypted storage, network isolation, and restricted access controls.

Limited Data Access

Only authorized staff and systems can access call logs and configuration data required to operate your service.

Call Logging & Monitoring

Logs and summaries help detect issues, improve accuracy, and ensure reliable escalation behavior.

ADA-Aware Call Handling

Call flows are structured to follow ADA-aligned service animal handling and reduce legal risk.

Ongoing Improvements

We continuously refine security practices, infrastructure monitoring, and system reliability.
