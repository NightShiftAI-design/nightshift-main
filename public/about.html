About NightShift

Built by someone who actually runs hotels.

NightShift wasn’t born in a software incubator. It came from years of watching family members answer calls at 1–3 AM, juggle walk-ins, and still try to act friendly to every guest.

Why NightShift exists

I grew up in and around hospitality. Every family gathering included at least one phone call from a guest, a vendor, or someone asking if they could check in “just a little bit early”.

Over the years I watched owners and managers lose sleep, miss dinners, and burn out trying to cover phones at all hours. Hiring a full night staff is expensive. Outsourcing to massive call centers often feels impersonal and disconnected from how independents actually run.

NightShift was built to solve that very specific pain: giving independent owners their nights back, without sacrificing bookings or guest experience.

How we work with you

  • We start small – usually one property and limited hours – so you can hear NightShift in action.
  • We configure policies and edge cases based on your real-world experience, not generic documentation.
  • We review early call summaries together and fine-tune tone, routing, and rules.
  • We grow coverage only when it feels like a net positive for you and your team.
