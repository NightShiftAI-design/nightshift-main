About NightShift AI

Built for Hotels. Designed for Reliability. Powered by AI.

NightShift AI replaces overnight and overflow front desk calls with a calm, professional, hotel-trained voice assistant that never sleeps. Built with real hotel operations in mind, NightShift ensures every call is answered, every booking is captured, and every guest feels supported.

Our Mission

Hotels lose thousands each year from missed calls, understaffed overnight shifts, and inconsistent front-desk coverage — especially independents and limited-service brands. NightShift AI solves this by providing:

  • Instant responses 24/7
  • Accurate, property-specific answers
  • Consistent guest experience
  • Reservation capture without human labor
  • Professional call handling that never burns out

The goal is simple: improve guest satisfaction while reducing operational strain, allowing hotels to run leaner without sacrificing service.

Why NightShift Exists

Many properties struggle with staffing shortages, rising labor costs, and unreliable overnight coverage. NightShift AI was created to ensure no hotel is ever left without a front desk voice — even for one minute.
