Privacy Policy
        

How NightShift AI Handles Your Data

        

          Last updated: January 2025             
          This Privacy Policy explains how NightShift AI (“NightShift,” “we,” “us,” or “our”)           collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, dashboard, and AI call-handling services           (collectively, the “Service”).         

      
    
         
      
                 
          

1. Information We Collect

          

            We collect information necessary to operate NightShift and deliver services to hotels,             motels, portfolios, and professional businesses (such as law firms).               This may include:           

          
                
  • Account & Business Information – name, email, phone number, hotel or business name, and role.
    •             
  • Configuration Data – property address, rates, fees, policies, amenities, and script preferences.
    •             
  • Call Metadata – caller phone number, call time, duration, call type, and high-level outcomes.
    •             
  • Call Content – transcripts or summaries generated by the AI to improve accuracy and provide logs.
    •             
  • Reservation Details – guest name, arrival date, nights, room type, guest count, and notes (no card numbers).
    •             
  • Website & Usage Data – pages visited, browser type, device type, and general usage analytics.
    •           
          

2. How We Use This Information

          

            We use collected data to:           

          
                
  • Operate and maintain the NightShift AI Service
    •             
  • Handle and route calls to or from your property
    •             
  • Create and send call summaries and reservation notifications
    •             
  • Improve AI performance, accuracy, and reliability
    •             
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues
    •             
  • Monitor for abuse, fraud, and security risks
    •           
          

3. Payment Information

          

            NightShift AI does not accept or store guests’ credit or debit card numbers through             AI voice agents.               Any payment processing for your subscription or setup fees is handled through secure third-party payment processors.           

          

4. Cookies & Analytics

          

            Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use NightShift.ai             and to improve the overall experience.             These may collect anonymous or aggregated usage data and do not contain payment information.           

        
                 
          

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

          

            We do not sell or rent your data.               We may share limited information with:           

          
                
  • Service Providers – cloud hosting, telephony, analytics, and support tools used to operate NightShift.
    •             
  • Integration Partners – such as PMS or CRM systems, only when you choose to connect them.
    •             
  • Legal or Regulatory Authorities – when required to comply with applicable law or valid legal requests.
    •           
          

6. Guest & Caller Data

          

            For hotels and businesses using NightShift:           

          
                
  • We process guest and caller data on your behalf as a service provider.
    •             
  • We use this data only to provide the Service, such as handling calls and sending summaries.
    •             
  • We do not use your guests’ information for independent marketing or resale.
    •           
          

7. Data Security

          

            We use commercially reasonable security measures to protect data, including:           

          
                
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
    •             
  • Access controls and authentication
    •             
  • Restricted access to internal tools
    •             
  • Separation of production and testing environments
    •           
          

            No system is 100% secure, but we are committed to continuously improving our security posture.           

          

8. PCI & ADA Alignment

          

            NightShift is designed to:           

          
                
  • Avoid collecting payment card numbers over the phone
    •             
  • Follow ADA-compliant service animal questioning patterns
    •             
  • Respect your hotel or business policies on pets, fees, and guest communication
    •           
        
      
    
         
      

9. Data Retention

      

        We retain call logs, summaries, and configuration data only as long as needed to deliver the Service         and support your business.           At your request, we may delete or anonymize certain data, subject to legal and operational requirements.       

      

10. Your Rights

      

        Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:       

      
            
  • Access the information we hold about you
    •         
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
    •         
  • Request deletion of certain data
    •         
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing
    •         
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
    •       
      

        To exercise any of these rights, contact us at           support@nightshifthotels.com.       

      

11. Children’s Privacy

      

        NightShift AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data         from minors.           If you believe a child has provided data through our Service, please contact us so we can remove it.       

      

12. Changes to This Policy

      

        We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Service or applicable laws.         When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.       

      

13. Contact Us

      

        If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, data handling, or your rights, please contact us at:
        support@nightshifthotels.com
