Privacy Policy

How NightShift AI Handles Your Data

Last updated: January 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how NightShift AI (“NightShift,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, dashboard, and AI call-handling services (collectively, the “Service”).

Summary

Key points

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We use data to operate the Service and support your business.
  • NightShift is designed to avoid collecting guest payment card numbers over calls.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.
Policy details

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate NightShift and deliver services to hotels, motels, portfolios, and professional businesses (such as law firms). This may include:

  • Account & Business Information – name, email, phone number, hotel/business name, and role.
  • Configuration Data – address, rates, fees, policies, amenities, and script preferences.
  • Call Metadata – caller number, call time, duration, call type, and high-level outcomes.
  • Call Content – transcripts or summaries generated by the AI for logs and improvement.
  • Reservation Details – guest name, arrival date, nights, room type, guest count, notes (no card numbers).
  • Website & Usage Data – pages visited, browser/device type, and general analytics.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service
  • Handle and route calls to/from your property
  • Create and send call summaries and notifications
  • Improve accuracy, reliability, and performance
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues
  • Monitor for abuse, fraud, and security risks

3. Payment Information

NightShift AI does not accept or store guests’ credit/debit card numbers through AI voice agents. Subscription/setup payments are handled through secure third-party payment processors.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site and improve the experience. These may collect anonymous or aggregated usage data and do not contain payment information.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers – hosting, telephony, analytics, and support tools used to operate NightShift.
  • Integration Partners – such as PMS/CRM systems, only when you choose to connect them.
  • Legal/Regulatory Authorities – when required to comply with law or valid requests.

6. Guest & Caller Data

For hotels and businesses using NightShift:

  • We process guest/caller data on your behalf as a service provider.
  • We use this data only to provide the Service (handling calls, logging outcomes, sending summaries).
  • We do not use guest information for independent marketing or resale.

7. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect data, including secure cloud infrastructure, access controls, and restricted internal access. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our security posture.

8. PCI & ADA Alignment

NightShift is designed to:

  • Avoid collecting payment card numbers over the phone
  • Follow ADA-aligned service animal handling patterns
  • Respect your policies on pets, fees, and guest communication
More details

9. Data Retention

We retain call logs, summaries, and configuration data only as long as needed to deliver the Service and support your business. At your request, we may delete or anonymize certain data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your data, and to restrict or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact support@nightshifthotels.com.

11. Children’s Privacy

NightShift AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us so we can remove it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Service or applicable laws. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your rights? Contact: support@nightshifthotels.com
