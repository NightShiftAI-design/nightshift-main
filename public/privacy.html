Privacy Policy
        

How We Protect Your Information

        

          Last updated: January 2025             
          NightShift AI is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency in how we collect, use, and secure information.         

      
    
         
      
                 
          

1. Information We Collect

          

            NightShift AI collects limited information necessary to operate and improve our services.             This includes:           

          
                
  • Hotel configuration details (rates, amenities, policies)
    •             
  • Call metadata (time, duration, caller number)
    •             
  • Reservation details submitted by callers
    •             
  • Messages sent via our contact forms
    •             
  • Basic analytics to improve performance
    •           
          

2. How We Use Your Information

          

We use data solely to operate the NightShift AI platform:

          
                
  • Routing calls and generating responses
    •             
  • Sending reservation summaries to hotel staff
    •             
  • Improving the accuracy and reliability of AI models
    •             
  • Providing support and troubleshooting
    •             
  • Maintaining security and preventing misuse
    •           
          

3. Data Security

          

            NightShift AI uses industry-standard security practices, including:           

          
                
  • Encrypted databases
    •             
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
    •             
  • No storage of credit or debit card numbers
    •             
  • Controlled access to sensitive information
    •           
        
                 
          

4. PCI & ADA Compliance

          

            NightShift AI follows strict PCI rules. Our agents never accept card numbers or payment details.             The system also adheres to ADA-approved service animal questioning standards.           

          

5. Data Sharing

          

            We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties except:           

          
                
  • When required to deliver the NightShift service
    •             
  • To comply with legal obligations
    •             
  • If you request integrations with PMS or third-party platforms
    •           
          

6. Hotel Guest Data

          

            Guest names, dates, and reservation details are transmitted securely to the hotel.             NightShift does not use guest data for marketing, profiling, or resale.           

          

7. Your Rights

          

            Depending on your region, you may have rights to:           

          
                
  • Access or correct your data
    •             
  • Request deletion of certain information
    •             
  • Limit processing of specific data fields
    •             
  • Request a full data export
    •           
        
      
    
         
      

8. Data Retention

      

        We retain call metadata and reservation information only as long as required for hotel operations.         Data may be deleted earlier upon request from property ownership.       

      

9. Children's Privacy

      

        NightShift AI is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from minors.       

      

10. Updates to This Policy

      

        We may update this Privacy Policy as NightShift evolves.         Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
