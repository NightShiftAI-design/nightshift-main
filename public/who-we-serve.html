Who we serve

Independent owners who hate missing calls.

NightShift is built for properties where one missed phone call is the difference between an empty room and another night of revenue.

Independent hotels & motels

25–90 room roadside properties, suburban hotels, and small boutique independents who can’t justify a full overnight staff but still need calls answered.

Small hotel groups

Owners with 2–6 properties who want a consistent after-hours experience across locations without turning everything over to a call center.

Small law firms

Firms that need every inbound inquiry captured professionally after hours, with a clean intake summary the team can act on the next morning.

For hospitality, we focus on…

  • Independent motel & hotel owners who still pick up the phone themselves at night.
  • Properties where front desk is often one person juggling walk-ins, phones, and late arrivals.
  • Owners who are tired of “are you open?”, “do you have parking?”, and “can I smoke in the room?” calls.

For law firms, NightShift…

  • Captures basic intake details for potential clients after hours.
  • Identifies urgent vs non-urgent matters so you know what to triage first.
  • Sends a clear intake summary to the right email list or phone.
