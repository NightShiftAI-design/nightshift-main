How it works

NightShift plugs into your existing phone number.

No hardware. No new numbers for guests. You simply forward after-hours calls to NightShift, and it behaves like a fully trained night front desk agent.

  1. Forward your main line after certain hours or when your staff is busy.
  2. NightShift answers as your property with your policies, room types, and pricing rules.
  3. AI handles the entire call – questions, bookings, directions, pet rules, emergencies.
  4. Instant summary is sent by email + SMS to the GM or owner, and stored in your dashboard.
