Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Hotels, motels, and management groups ask these questions the most. NightShift AI is designed to be simple, fast to install, and compliant.

How fast can we get NightShift installed?

Most properties go live quickly. We collect your rates, policies, and routing preferences, then deploy your trained agent.

Does NightShift follow ADA rules for service animals?

Yes. NightShift follows ADA-aligned handling and avoids asking for documentation or proof.

Can NightShift book rooms?

Yes. It can collect dates, quote rates, calculate totals with taxes/fees, apply your policies, and confirm bookings based on your workflow.

Is this compliant with PCI?

NightShift can be configured to avoid collecting card numbers and instead route payments through your existing secure process.

What if a caller has an emergency?

NightShift can be configured to prompt callers to contact emergency services and escalate to staff based on your rules. It does not provide medical or legal advice.

Can NightShift match or view online rates?

NightShift does not browse OTA systems, but it can follow your policy for rate matching or escalate to the front desk/manager.

Which hotel brands can use NightShift?

NightShift supports independent properties and can be tailored for brand environments with brand-safe scripting and terminology.

Can we customize our script?

Yes. Your script can include address, amenities, fees, rates, smoking policy, pet rules, brand language, and staff contacts.

Does NightShift speak other languages?

NightShift can support multiple languages (like Spanish, Hindi, Gujarati, and more) based on your setup.

What happens if NightShift can’t answer a question?

It escalates cleanly to your staff (front desk/GM) based on your routing rules — no loops, no awkward dead-ends.

How does NightShift handle weekly rates?

NightShift can present nightly vs weekly pricing and follow your exact quoting logic.

Is this replacing staff?

No — it supports your team by handling repetitive calls, reducing missed bookings, and improving response time after hours.
