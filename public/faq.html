Top questions

The most common questions

If you don’t see your question here, send a quote request — we’ll reply quickly.

How fast can we get NightShift installed?

Many properties can go live quickly. We collect your property details (rates, policies, escalation contacts), configure the workflows, and test call handling before you forward your after-hours line.

Is NightShift a call center?

No. NightShift is a virtual front desk trained on your hotel’s policies and procedures. It’s built for guest operations (questions, issues, late arrivals, escalation) — not just reservations.

Can NightShift take reservations?

Yes. It can capture dates, nights, guest count, and room type, then follow your booking workflow. If you prefer, reservation attempts can be routed to staff for confirmation.

Do you collect credit card numbers?

NightShift can be configured to avoid collecting card numbers and instead route payments through your existing secure process (front desk workflow, payment link, or your preferred method).

How does NightShift handle service animals and ADA-sensitive situations?

NightShift can be configured with ADA-aware workflows and consistent handling. For sensitive scenarios, you can require escalation to staff so a human makes the final decision.

What if a caller has an emergency?

NightShift is designed to recognize urgent situations and escalate immediately to your designated contacts. It can also instruct callers to contact emergency services when appropriate.

What if NightShift can’t answer a question?

It escalates cleanly to your staff based on routing rules you control — with context, not confusion. No loops, no dead ends, and no “try calling back later.”

Can we customize the scripts and policies?

Yes. We configure your property info and policies: fees, deposits, smoking rules, pets, late check-in procedures, quiet hours, and escalation rules — tailored to your operation.

Does NightShift support multiple languages?

Multi-language support can be enabled based on your setup (for example: Spanish, Hindi, Gujarati and more). The caller experience stays professional and hospitality-focused.

Can NightShift match online rates or check OTAs?

NightShift doesn’t browse OTAs. If a guest mentions a rate, NightShift can follow your policy (explain, offer your approved rate, or escalate to staff).

How does NightShift handle weekly rates and extended stays?

NightShift can present nightly vs weekly pricing and follow your quoting logic, including deposits and policy language you specify.

Is this replacing staff?

NightShift replaces overnight call handling — not your entire front desk. Routine calls get resolved automatically, and sensitive situations get escalated to a human based on your rules. The goal is fewer overnight staffing hours and fewer missed calls.
