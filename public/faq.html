FAQ

Common questions from owners & managers.

Does NightShift replace my front desk?

No. NightShift is focused on nights and low-volume hours. During the day, it can assist but humans stay in control.

What if a guest is very upset or it’s an emergency?

NightShift is trained to recognize high-urgency calls and escalate to your chosen on-call number or instructions.

How long does setup take?

Most properties are live within 1–2 days after a quick intake call and a few test calls together.

Can guests tell it’s AI?

Most just assume it’s a very efficient night auditor. The goal is not to “sound like a robot”, but to be helpful and clear.

Do you lock us into long contracts?

No. We believe NightShift should pay for itself quickly. If it doesn’t, you shouldn’t be stuck in a long agreement.

How does pricing work for multiple properties?

We start with one property and then discount as you roll out to others. Reach out and we’ll tailor it to your portfolio.
