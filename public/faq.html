Top questions

The most common questions

If you don’t see your question here, send a quote request — we’ll reply quickly.

How fast can we get NightShift installed?

Most properties go live quickly. We collect your property details (rates, policies, escalation contacts), configure your script, and test the call flow before you forward your line.

Does NightShift follow ADA rules for service animals?

Yes — NightShift is configured to follow ADA-aligned handling and avoids asking for documentation or proof. Your pet fees and service animal rules are applied exactly as you specify.

Can NightShift book rooms?

Yes. It can collect dates, nights, guest count, and room type, then follow your booking workflow. If you want bookings to route to staff for confirmation, we can do that too.

Is this PCI compliant?

NightShift can be configured to avoid collecting card numbers and instead route payments through your existing secure process (front desk, payment link, or your preferred method).

What if a caller has an emergency?

NightShift uses safe emergency handling language and can route urgent calls based on your rules. It does not provide medical or legal advice.

Can NightShift match or view online rates?

NightShift doesn’t browse OTAs. If a guest mentions a rate, we can follow your policy (match, explain, or escalate to the front desk/manager).

Which hotel brands can use NightShift?

NightShift supports independent properties and can be tailored for franchise environments with brand-safe scripting, terminology, and structured call flows.

Can we customize the script?

Yes. Your script can include address, amenities, fees, rates, smoking policy, pet rules, late check-in procedures, and staff contact rules — all tailored to your property.

Does NightShift speak other languages?

Yes — multi-language support can be enabled based on your setup (e.g., Spanish, Hindi, Gujarati, and more), with a professional tone in each language.

What happens if NightShift can’t answer a question?

It escalates cleanly to your staff (front desk/GM) based on your routing rules — no loops, no dead ends, and no confusing handoffs.

How does NightShift handle weekly rates?

NightShift can present nightly vs weekly pricing and follow your exact quoting logic, including deposits, taxes/fees, and your policy language.

Is this replacing staff?

No — it supports your team by handling repetitive calls, reducing missed bookings, and improving response time after hours so staff can focus on guests in person.
