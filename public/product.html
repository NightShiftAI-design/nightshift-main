Product

The AI night front desk built for independents.

NightShift isn’t a generic chatbot dropped into your phones. It’s a fully trained, hotel-aware voice agent that behaves like your most reliable night auditor.

What NightShift actually does on a call

  • Answers with your hotel name, tone, and greeting.
  • Understands room types, occupancy, dates, taxes, pet fees, smoking rules, and cash bookings.
  • Responds to common questions: rates, directions, parking, Wi-Fi, breakfast, check-in / out times.
  • Politely filters obvious nuisance or unsafe callers.
  • Escalates real emergencies directly to your team.
  • Sends a clean summary of every call to your email + SMS.
Natural, human-like voice
Understands accents & noisy environments
No scripts for you to maintain

Call summary structure

Every call is logged using a simple, owner-friendly format:

  • Caller type – new guest, in-house, vendor, nonsense, emergency.
  • Outcome – booked, info only, escalated, blocked.
  • Key details – dates, room type, rate, cash/card, notes.
  • Action needed – “no action”, “hold room 102”, “call guest back”.
Under the hood

Trained on hospitality, not just generic support.

NightShift is tuned around how independent hotels and motels actually run: cash guests, local events, long-stay discounts, and guests who call at 1:30 AM angry or lost.

Hospitality-aware knowledge

We encode your policies, local directions, common edge cases, and the way your property really works – not just what’s on your website.

Safe workflows by design

No card details are ever collected over the phone. Guests are guided to secure payment links or front desk processes you already use.

Human handoff any time

NightShift can route calls to a manager or on-call number when needed, and clearly mark which calls require human follow-up.
